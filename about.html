About Safisha Laundry

Safisha Laundry was created to simplify laundry management for individuals and businesses by offering an efficient, user-friendly inventory tracking system.

Inspiration Behind the Project

The idea for Safisha Laundry came from recognizing the challenges laundry businesses face in tracking clothes, managing customer orders, and keeping up with overdue collections. The goal was to create a simple and intuitive web application that helps laundry service providers streamline their operations effortlessly.

Meet the Creators

Safisha Laundry was developed by Kelvin Mabonga and Tom Morara who are passionate about technology and automation. With a background in software engineering and a keen interest in building web applications, the creators aimed to develop a solution that brings efficiency and convenience to the laundry industry.

We believe that technology should simplify everyday tasks, and Safisha Laundry is our contribution to making laundry management seamless and hassle-free. This is part of our CHAIR Values at ALX where we are inspired to provide solutions to the problems facing our communities
